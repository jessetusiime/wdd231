Swift Auto Gallery

Website Siteplan – Car Models and Specifications Gallery

Site Name & Branding

Site Name: Swift Auto Gallery

Reason: Swift Auto Gallery is a combination of speed, professionalism, and clarity. The site conveys efficiency and quick access to information that is perfect for users who want fast, easy browsing of car specifications.

Site Purpose

Swift Auto Gallery is a professional, information-focused car gallery that serves as a dynamic showcase of diverse vehicle models and their detailed specifications. The site provides:

  • A browsable collection of 15+ car models with specifications (brand, model, year, engine type, horsepower)
  • Quick-view cards with essential information and the ability to access full details through modals
  • A responsive, user-friendly experience optimized for both desktop and mobile browsing
  • Dynamic content management through JSON data integration, ensuring consistency and scalability
  • Educational context about how the site leverages modern web technologies (JavaScript, localStorage, responsive design)

The site targets car enthusiasts, potential buyers researching vehicles, and anyone seeking reliable, well-organized automotive specification data.

Target Audience & User Scenarios

Primary Audience:

Car enthusiasts and potential buyers seeking clear, organized information about various vehicle models. Users value easy navigation, comprehensive specs, and the ability to compare vehicles easily.

Secondary Audience:

Researchers, students, and general information seekers who want quick access to reliable car specifications without confusion or sales pressure.

User Scenarios:

Scenario 1: The Comparison buyer

"I'm looking to buy a new car and want to compare 3–4 different models quickly. I want to see side by side specs like horsepower, engine type, and year without having to click through multiple pages."

Solution: The Models page displays a dynamic grid of car cards with essential specs that are visible. Users can click "View Details" to open a modal with the information, allowing easy comparison without leaving the page.

Scenario 2: The Common Enthusiast

"I'm browsing on my phone during and want to explore interesting cars. I'd like to see featured vehicles, learn about their specs, and maybe save my favorites for later."

Solution: The home page features a selection of standout vehicles, and the responsive design adapts to mobile. localStorage tracks their favorite cars, allowing users to revisit their saved selections on different visits.

Scenario 3: The First Time Visitor

"I'm new to the site. I want to understand its purpose, how the data is organized, and what makes this different from other car sites."

Solution: The About page clearly explains the site's mission, data structure, and the technical approach (JSON integration, JavaScript rendering). A brief creator bio builds trust and transparency.

Color Usage:

  • Primary Blue (#1E88E5): Main navigation bar, primary CTA buttons, section accents, hover states, and key interactive elements.
  • Dark Blue (#0D47A1): Page headers, main headings, section titles, and footer background for depth and hierarchy.
  • Light Gray (#F5F5F5): Page background, card backgrounds, section separators, and subtle contrast areas.
  • Dark Text (#333333): Body copy, paragraphs, and all readable content for optimal contrast and readability.
  • White (#FFFFFF): Card backgrounds, content boxes, and primary content areas to maintain visual separation.

Typography

For typography, two fonts will be used Montserrat and Roboto

Montserrat (Bold)
Usage: Page titles (H1), section headings (H2, H3), navigation links, and all labels.
Roboto Regular / Medium
Usage: Body text, paragraphs, card descriptions, and form labels.

Font Hierarchy:

  • H1 (Montserrat 2.5rem, bold): Page title/site name
  • H2 (Montserrat 1.8rem, bold): Major section headings
  • H3 (Montserrat 1.3rem, bold): Subsection headings
  • Body (Roboto 1rem, regular): Paragraphs and descriptive text
  • Small (Roboto 0.85rem): Labels, metadata, and auxiliary text
